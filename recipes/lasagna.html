Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • Ragù
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Vegetables, cheeses
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Cook the Noodles. Cooks the lasagna noodles in boiling salted water.

  2. Lasagna Sauce. Dice four big tomatoes and cook them so the juice of the tomato begins to leak.

  3. Layering. Add a layer of ricotta cheese on the bottom of the pan.

  4. Finishing Touch.

  5. Bake.
